About Me

From a young age I loved to write. I created by first childrens book at just six years old about a dancing hamster and his rise to stardom, it even had an about the author page! This passion for writing led me to believe that I wanted to be an author when I grow up, but after taking my first journalism class in high school the path I imagined for my future changed. The fact driven writing style of the journalism field combined with creative storytelling made certain that journalism was the right path for me. I still love telling stories, but today I work to tell the true stories of those in the community I serve.
